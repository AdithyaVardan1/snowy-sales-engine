
1. The 5-Channel Zero-Budget Sales Engine
Every channel below targets one audience: people who want to run OpenClaw but don’t want to deal with servers. That’s the entire strategy.

Channel 1: OpenClaw Community Infiltration (60% of effort)
The OpenClaw community is 225K+ strong and growing daily. They’re active on GitHub Discussions (the primary forum), Discord, Reddit (r/openclaw, r/selfhosted, r/LocalLLaMA), Telegram groups, and Twitter. This is where our customers live. We need to be there every single day:
GitHub Discussions — Answer EVERY hosting question: 
Search for questions about deployment, Docker issues, VPS setup, SSL errors, memory problems, and crash logs. Answer them genuinely and helpfully. At the end of your answer, add: “If you’d rather skip the setup entirely, I built Snowy AI — it’s managed OpenClaw hosting that gets you running in 60 seconds.” Do NOT spam. Give real value first. The ratio should be 90% help, 10% mention.
Discord — Become a recognized helper: 
Join the OpenClaw Discord. Spend 1-2 hours daily helping people in #support and #deployment channels. Answer questions about configuration, skills, integrations. After 2-3 weeks of consistent helping, you’ll be recognized by the community. Then you can share Snowy AI naturally when people ask about hosting.
Reddit — Post detailed comparison guides: 
Write genuine, detailed posts: “I compared every way to host OpenClaw — here’s what I found.” Include self-hosting, VPS, Docker, and managed options (including competitors). Be honest about trade-offs. Reddit rewards authenticity and punishes shilling. A genuine comparison that includes Snowy AI as one option will outperform any advertisement.
GitHub Issues — Contribute actual code: 
Submit PRs to the OpenClaw repo. Fix bugs, improve docs, write skills. Being a contributor gives you credibility that no marketing can buy. When a contributor recommends their hosting product, it carries 100x the weight of a random ad.
Create the “OpenClaw Hosting FAQ” resource: 
Build a free, comprehensive guide: “Every way to host OpenClaw in 2026, ranked.” Cover free tiers, $5 VPS, Docker, managed hosts. Make it genuinely useful. This becomes a resource people share organically. Host it on your blog and let it rank in Google.

4: Product-Led Growth & Viral Mechanics

The product itself is the most scalable acquisition channel. Build virality into the core experience from day one:
7-Day Free Trial (No Credit Card): 
Remove all friction from the first experience. Let people deploy an agent for free for 7 days. The product sells itself once they experience the “60 seconds to running agent” magic. Most will convert when they realize the alternative is 2 hours of Docker + VPS setup.
‘Powered by Snowy AI’ in Agent Messages: 
On the free trial (and optionally the Starter tier), every message the agent sends includes a subtle footer: “Powered by Snowy AI — Run OpenClaw in 60 seconds.” Every person who interacts with the agent sees this. If an agent is in a group chat with 50 people, that’s 50 impressions per message.
‘Invite a Friend, Get a Month Free’: 
Simple referral: if someone you refer subscribes, you both get one free month. At $25/month, this costs you $25 to acquire a $300/year customer (8.3% CAC). That’s exceptional unit economics.
Public Agent Gallery: 
Let users optionally showcase their agent’s capabilities on a public gallery page. “See what people are building with Snowy AI.” Each gallery entry is a landing page that ranks for its use case + attracts curious visitors.
Onboarding NPS Survey at Day 3: 
Send an in-app survey 3 days after signup: “How likely are you to recommend Snowy AI?” Anyone who answers 9-10 gets an automatic prompt: “Would you share Snowy AI with a friend? Here’s your referral link.” Capture promoters at peak satisfaction.

These creators need content ideas — “Set up an AI agent on WhatsApp in 60 seconds” is a great video for their channels.
Telegram Bot / WhatsApp Tool Communities: 
There are thousands of Telegram and WhatsApp groups dedicated to bots and automation. Join them. Help people. Share Snowy AI when relevant. In many of these groups, members are actively looking for AI assistant solutions.

Attend (free) local hackathons and dev meetups. Demo Snowy AI live: “Watch me deploy an AI agent in 60 seconds.” The speed is the demo. Collect emails, offer trial codes. One good hackathon can generate 20-50 trials.
